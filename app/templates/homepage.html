Instruction

How to use this website?
Step 1: Press "Sign Up" and fill all required information.
The username length must be between 6 and 15 characters.
The password must be between 6 and 15 characters, has at least 1 uppercase character, 1 lowercase character and 1 number character.
Password confirmation must be same as the entered password.
You can press the "eye" icon to show and hide your password. After that, press "Login" and welcome to the traffic detection website.
Press "START" to access the process link.
Step 2: Press "Choose File" button and upload your images (only .png, .jpeg, .jpg, .gif, .tiff, .bmp are accepted)
Press "Process" to start detecting traffic objects inside images.
Class lists can be detected are: road, sidewalk, building, wall, fence, pole, traffic light, traffic sign, vegetation, terrain, sky, person, rider, car, truck, bus, train, motorcycle, bicycle.
After processed images appear, press two options to download to your computer:
- Download Process Image: download image with boxes and confident scores.
- Download Detected Info (CSV): download detail file, include image size, running time, each object confident score.
